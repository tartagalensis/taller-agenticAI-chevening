## How to work with me

- **Plan before acting.** Propose a short plan and wait for my OK before
  reading many files, writing new files, or running commands.
- **Do not invent facts, numbers, or citations.** If you are not sure,
  say so. If a claim needs a source and no source is in `docs/`, flag it.
- **Sources live in `docs/`.** Do not modify them — they are read-only.
- **Output goes to `output/`.** Use clear filenames.
- **Ask before changing scope.** If you think the task should be bigger
  or smaller than what I described above, ask first.
- **Be concise.** Short answers are usually better than long ones.
